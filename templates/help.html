Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password, simply click Forgot Password in the account settings. You will be prompted to enter your email belonging to your account to reset your password.
You may buy stocks from the dashboard or buy stock page. Simply click on a stock you would like to buy and view information about the stock. From here, you can press the green "Buy" button followed by the quantity of stock(s) you would like to purchase.
Users can sell their current holdings on the Sell Page located from the dashboard. Users clicking on this page will be able to access all of their holdings and confirm specific holdings to sell.
Simply locate the "Trade History" icon from the dashboard. Users can view all trades they have ever made here.
This application does not use real money. User's can deposit any amount of fake money as they please for stock teaching purposes
